Marking a lecture with PodCats
1. Quickmark - when PodCatsing a lecture, simply press the lightning bolt button to quickly mark a point in lecture. The only information that will be saved with a quickmark is the time at which you pressed it.
2. Bookmark - pressing the regular bookmark button will allow you to mark an interval in the lecture. The first click will open up the comment dialog to add any details you want to be associated with the marked interval. A second click to end the mark will save your marked interval.
